Featurelist

  • Supports 111 different types of modules, including Protracker, SID, IT, S3M, MP3, OGG and Fasttracker modules.
  • One of the most accurate players in the world: plays modules like nature intended.
  • Fast and optimized.
  • Edit and Save your favourite lists of modules with the Modulelist-editor.
  • See and play the modules samples on the keyboard.
  • Can load packed files.
  • Shows the module lenght, no matter what type it is.
  • Save your favorite modules as sample files.
